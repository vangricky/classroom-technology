Our Purpose

As classroom technology technicians, we do our very best to maintain and fix our technology in each and every classroom on campus that are under our care. We have been professionally trained to understand each system in a classroom to our best knowledge to help aid you in fixing your room that may be having technical issues. We understand how important technology is relied on during a class to help you teach. We are here to help you and your students have the best experience at Brigham Young University-Idaho.
